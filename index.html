Pokemon Quiz for Pokemon Trainers

Rules

This is a quiz game about the fictional world of Pokemon and the characters and events in it.
You shall be given a question and will answer the question by choosing one of three answer's below. There are a total of 20 questions and will be based on the very first pokemon anime that revolve around Pokemon trainer Ashe's first journey.
There is a score box that will display how many questions you have gotten correct and how many incorrect answer's.
There is also a running timer next to your score so you can improve your speed at answering correctly. You will also have the option to restart the game if you would like to.
But good luck, have fun and try to be the very best, like no one ever was!!
